## Background Design

### Base Color
- **Vintage newspaper beige**: #e8e4d9
- Warm, aged paper appearance
- Easy on the eyes
- Professional look

### Layered Effects

#### Layer 1: Grid Pattern
- Subtle 50px grid lines
- Creates newspaper column effect
- Very light opacity (3%)

#### Layer 2: Diagonal Crosshatch
- 45° and -45° diagonal lines
- Adds texture and depth
- Newspaper print texture simulation

#### Layer 3: Newspaper Text
- Actual news keywords floating in background
- "BREAKING NEWS • DAILY EXPRESS • THE TIMES • WORLD NEWS"
- Rotated -3° for dynamic feel
- Times New Roman font (classic newspaper)
- 12% opacity (subtle but visible)
